Who We Are

Our Leadership

For over 25 years, David Neely has been honored to serve the St. Petersburg community as Owner/Operator of Chick-fil-A 4th Street North. A third generation native of St. Petersburg, David started his Chick-fil-A career at the age of 25; one of the youngest to be selected as a franchise owner. With his vision to bring the Chick-fil-A free standing concept to St. Petersburg, David opened up his 4th Street restaurant in March 1999; the first in Pinellas County and St. Petersburg.

Throughout the years, his hard work and passion for hospitality has awarded him the Chick-fil-A, Inc. Symbol of Success, Chick-fil-A’s highest honor for an Operator, recognizing those who successfully achieve aggressive sales goals.

While David’s role serves as the CEO; providing Vision, Clarity and Focus, he prefers to spend his time serving his team and community through mentorship and leadership development. His goal is to help Team Members achieve their goals. He is passionate about using his resources to serve the St. Petersburg community, making it a wonderful place to live and work.

Outside of work, David enjoys reading, classic movies, golf, running and travel. A perfect day for David is spending time with his wife Michelle, their three adult children and Bates Neely (The Worlds Best Labrador).
