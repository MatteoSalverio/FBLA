Why Work With Us?

Employee Value Proposition

Working at Chick-fil-A Fourth Street is more than just a job, it’s a place where you can make a difference every day, while growing as a person and a professional.


Our Purpose

To make St. Petersburg a better place.



Employee Benefits

FLEXIBILITY

Whether our team members are involved in athletics, in community service, or just need some family time, we value flexibility of schedule for our team. We are closed on Sundays to allow our team members to rest and enjoy time with family and friends.

COMPETITIVE PAY

We value your contribution to our organization and provide competitive compensation within the hospitality industry. Furthermore, we encourage employees to take initiative in their own advancement through extraordinary work ethic and leadership demonstration.

GROWTH AND DEVELOPMENT

We value advancement via performance reviews, the opportunity to earn leadership responsibilities and the opportunity to enter into mentoring relationships with leaders.

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

We promise the opportunity to educate and assist our team members in career opportunities which include, but are not limited to, Franchisee, Support Center Staff, contracting with Chick-fil-A and long-term career opportunities with RRM.



Plus, employees may be eligible for the following benefits:

  • Employee meals
  • Catering discounts
  • Health insurance
  • Paid time off
  • College scholarships
  • Performance incentives and more!
