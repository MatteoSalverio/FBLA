Not sure what you want to do?

Don't worry, we can help you!

Chick-fil-A has a wide range of available jobs. From working in the kitchen, to playing the role of our beloved mascot; we will find the perfect fit for you.


Front of House

$14/hr

In the front of house position, you will be the face of Chick-fil-A. You will be frequently interacting with our customers, and ensuring that they have the best experience possible. We have a vision that primarily involves hospitality, and it is up to you to make it happen.

Back of House

$14.50/hr

In the back of house position, you will help to create the meals that people love, and ensure high quality for every customer. Employees in this position will not directly interact with customers as much, but they still have a major impact on the overall customer experience.

Team Leader

$17/hr

As a Team Leader, you will be the role model for the rest of our team members. You will be showing them what it looks like to be a valuable member of our restaurant.
